Joko Permana

Joko Permana, born in 2005 and proudly based in Las Vegas, is not your average gaming enthusiast. He’s part of a new generation of gamers who grew up surrounded by screens, consoles, and the ever-evolving world of browser games.

From the moment he played his first 8-bit title, Joko was hooked. What started as curiosity in childhood soon became a passion—and eventually, a voice in the online gaming community.

Joko specializes in unblocked games and browser-based experiences. He loves games that are simple, fast-loading, and surprisingly addictive—just like Drift Boss Unblocked.

Whether it's testing the latest variations of drift boss 66, sharing honest game reviews, or helping fellow players find safe school-friendly options, Joko blends experience and insight into everything he writes.

As a long-time gamer, he believes that gaming should be for everyone, everywhere—no downloads, no paywalls, no limits. That’s why his blog content focuses on free access, fairness, and fun.

“I don’t just play games—I explore how they work, where they shine, and how to make them more enjoyable for others”, Joko shares.

Follow Joko Permana as he continues reviewing web-based titles, from racing hits like Drift Boss to platformer classics and upcoming indie gems. He’s always ready to drift, jump, or shoot his way through the next great game.
